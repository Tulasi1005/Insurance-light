Our History

Quintera (previously Star International Insurance Company) was born out of the insurance agency operations of Quintera Group & Company and commenced writing business on September 1, 1981.

As a wholly owned subsidiary of Quintera Group Limited, Quintera has embraced the parent company’s values of honesty, integrity, and trust.

In 1999 Quintera acquired the National Commercial Bank owned CHIC/NCB Insurance Company. In March 2005 Quintera acquired the Jamaican portfolio of Nova Insurance Company Limited. As a result, Quintera became the largest insurer in Jamaica in 2006 (based on premium volume).

Quintera has consistently ranked amongst the top three insurers in Jamaica based on profitability and strength of balance sheet.

Quintera is licensed in accordance with The Insurance Act (2001) to write all classes of General Insurance business, namely: -

  • Motor Vehicle Insurance
  • Property Insurance
  • Personal Accident Insurance
  • Pecuniary Loss Insurance
  • Marine, Aviation and Transport Insurance
  • Liability Insurance
  • Microinsurance

Quintera protects its security via reinsurance contracts with some of the world's largest and most highly rated reinsurers including Swiss Re, Munich Re, Transatlantic Re, SCOR, QBE, Mapfre, R+V Versicherungs and Lloyds.

Quintera has invested in technology to support its operation and are widely regarded as leaders in this area, Quintera was the first insurance company in Jamaica to introduce a truly interactive web-site, which allows brokers and clients to access policy, claims and information.

A focus on efficiency combined with a commitment to excellence and a highly trained workforce has earned Quintera an enviable reputation for efficiency & customer service.

Quintera's customers consistently indicate a high level of satisfaction with its products and services via internal and independent surveys. Since 2005 the customer satisfaction index has consistently remained above 90%.

We are proud of the reputation we have built in providing excellent service to our clients in the field of general insurance.
